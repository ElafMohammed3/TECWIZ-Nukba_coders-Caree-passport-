Interview Tips for Graduates & Professionals

Prepare for interviews by researching the company, practicing common questions, and developing compelling stories about your experiences and achievements.

Remember to dress appropriately, arrive on time, and follow up with a thank-you note after the interview.

Resume Guidelines for Graduates & Professionals

Create a compelling resume that highlights your skills, experiences, and achievements. Tailor your resume for each job application and use action verbs to describe your accomplishments.

Keep your resume concise, typically 1-2 pages, and ensure it is free of errors and easy to read.
